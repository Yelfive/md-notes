## CAP

- `C` Consistency
- `A` Available
- `P` Partition tolerance

**CAP theorem(AKA, Brewer's theorem)**

- `C`: Consistency
- `A` Available
- `P` Partition tolerance

> Distributed system can never fulfill all the three
